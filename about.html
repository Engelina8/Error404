Our mission

The poodcast '404: Security not Found' was created as part of our cybersecurity coursework with a simple mission: to break down complex cybersecurity concepts into digestible, actionable insights.

What we cover

  • Cybersecurity fundamentals and core concepts
  • Current threats and attack vectors
  • Security tools and technologies
  • Best practices for personal and organizational security
